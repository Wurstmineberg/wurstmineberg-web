USC An overview of past USCs

USC is short for Ultra SoftCore, and it's the name of our very own, slightly awkwardly named version of the UHC game style invented and popularized on the Mindcrack server. The rules vary from match to match (like on Mindcrack UHC), but the primary difference is that we give players another chance when they die from environmental damage (hostile mobs, fall damage, fire/lava). We mainly do that because we're exceedingly happen to even manage to get 4 people scheduled for a match, and having that match end after 30 minutes because someone derped up would kind of defeat the point of having a fun game.

USC III 2013-10-05

Participants: Team Aqua: Fenhl, Papierschiff, Jemus42, Team Light Purple: L3viathan, Naturalismus, Farthen

Rules: Since we managed to get a decent number of players this time (i.e. P > 4) and even had an even number, we went for a team deathmatch, 3v3 last team standing. Besides that Fenhl and L3viathan built a little USC-box: A redstone contraption to automate team randomization and the death timeout. The timeout wen this way: A dead player respawned in a bed in a bedrock box, where he could either leave the game if he died a genuine PvP death, or step on a pressure plate which teleported him on top of enough cobwebs for a 10 minute fall. After that fall he would be randomly teleported on a spot on the map via the /scatter command, thus ensuring fun times in terms of finding back to one's team while trying not to die horribly. It went okay.

Winners: Team Light Purple (L3viathan, Naturalismus, Farthen)
